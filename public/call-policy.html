Call Policy

Permitted Use

The TalkHint service may only be used for live voice conversations initiated by the user. All calls are handled in real-time with human participation. Users may receive AI-generated suggestions, but all responses are delivered manually by the user.

Prohibited Use

The service must not be used for robocalls, autodialing, marketing, promotional or bulk voice traffic. Calls that violate TCPA, local laws, or Twilio terms of service are strictly forbidden.

Compliance

TalkHint complies with applicable U.S. regulations, including TCPA. All calls are initiated by the user with consent and processed in real-time. No recordings or marketing calls are made. Learn more at https://talkhint.me.
