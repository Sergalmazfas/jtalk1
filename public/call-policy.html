Call Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

This policy describes how TalkHint, operated by All Digital Land LLC, uses phone numbers and ensures user consent.

1. Purpose of Numbers

Phone numbers provided by TalkHint are intended exclusively for real-time conversation assistance using AI-generated prompts.

2. Opt-In Consent

By calling or receiving calls via TalkHint numbers, you consent to:

  • Real-time speech recognition for prompt generation
  • AI-assisted response suggestions
  • Related SMS (if used)

This consent is granted upon use — no separate checkbox or written form is required. By using the number, you agree to this Call Policy.

3. Opt-Out / Withdrawal of Consent

To withdraw consent:

  • Simply stop using the number
  • Or email us to request deactivation

4. Data Handling

We do not store audio. Transcription and prompts are processed in real time only. No data is sold or used for marketing purposes.
